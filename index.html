Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Book a free call and we'll answer any question directly.

How much can I borrow? +

Borrowing power depends on your income, expenses, deposit, and which lender assesses your application. Book a free assessment for an accurate figure based on your specific situation.

Do you charge a broker fee? +

No. Our home loan services are paid by the lender when your loan settles, at no cost to you. Any fee, if applicable to your situation, is disclosed in full before you proceed.

How long does pre-approval take? +

Pre-approval can be 24 to 72 hours with the right documentation. Full approval typically completes within 2 to 6 weeks depending on the lender and property type.

Can you help self-employed applicants? +

Yes, self employed borrowers are one of our specialties. We work with lenders that correctly assess tax returns, BAS statements, and income add backs. Book a call to discuss your situation.

Do doctors qualify for special home loans? +

Yes. Eligible doctors, dentists, pharmacists and other medical professionals can access home loans up to 95% LVR with no LMI through select lenders, subject to eligibility. See our dedicated doctors page for details.
