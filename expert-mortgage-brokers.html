FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a mortgage broker cost?
In most cases, nothing. Mortgage brokers are paid a commission by the lender when your loan settles. You receive expert advice and lender comparison at no out-of-pocket cost to you.
How many lenders do you compare?
We compare home loan products from 40+ lenders including major banks, second-tier lenders, and specialist non-bank lenders to find options that may suit your situation.
How long does the home loan process take?
From initial consultation to formal approval typically takes 2–4 weeks, depending on the lender and complexity of your application.
Can you help first home buyers?
Yes. We help first home buyers understand their options, eligibility for government schemes, and navigate the application process from start to settlement.
Do you service all Melbourne eastern suburbs?
Yes. We primarily service Ringwood, Bayswater, Boronia, Wantirna, Heathmont, Bayswater North and surrounding eastern suburbs, as well as greater Melbourne.
What documents do I need to apply?
Typically: 2 recent payslips, last 2 years tax returns (if self-employed), 3 months bank statements, photo ID, and details of any existing debts or assets. We'll confirm exactly what's needed for your situation.
