Frequently asked

Contact & Consultation Questions

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours during business days. Most clients hear from us within a few hours.

Is there a cost to talk to a broker?

No. Our consultation is free. We're paid by the lender when your loan settles, not by you.

What information do I need to provide?

Just basic details to start — your name, contact info, and what you're looking to do (buy, refinance, invest). We'll ask for more detail once we understand your situation.

Do you only work with people in the eastern suburbs?

We're based in Melbourne's eastern suburbs, but we work with clients across Victoria. If you're outside this area, get in touch and we'll let you know if we can help.

Can I call instead of using the form?

Absolutely. Call 0419 891 983 and we'll chat through your situation right away.

What if I'm not ready to apply yet?

No problem. We're here to answer questions and help you understand your options, whether you're ready to move forward now or just exploring.
