The cost question

Does using a broker cost more?

No. This is the most common misconception. Mortgage brokers in Australia are paid a commission by the lender when your loan settles. There is no cost to you. You get access to 30+ lenders, personalised advice, and someone managing your application from start to settlement, for exactly the same price as walking into a bank branch: free.
