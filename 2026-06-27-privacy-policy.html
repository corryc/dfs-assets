Who We Are

Digital Finance Solutions is a mortgage broking business operating in Melbourne's eastern suburbs. We provide credit assistance services including home loans, investment property loans, refinancing, car loans, personal loans, and debt consolidation.

We act as a credit representative of Beagle Finance Pty Ltd (ACL 391237). Our Credit Representative Number is 532405.

Our Services

We provide credit assistance by comparing loan products from a panel of lenders and making recommendations based on your individual circumstances. We do not provide financial advice as defined under the Corporations Act 2001.

All information provided on our website and in our communications is general in nature and does not constitute financial or credit advice. You should consider whether our recommendations are appropriate for your personal circumstances.

How We Are Remunerated

We are remunerated by lenders when a loan we recommend is settled. This remuneration typically includes:

  • Upfront commission — a percentage of the loan amount paid by the lender at settlement
  • Trail commission — an ongoing percentage of the outstanding loan balance paid by the lender over the life of the loan

Commission rates vary by lender. In most circumstances, we do not charge you a direct fee for our services. If a fee is applicable, we will disclose this to you before providing any credit assistance.

A full credit guide detailing our remuneration will be provided to you when we first provide credit assistance.
